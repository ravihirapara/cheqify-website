Synthesis of data from Keyword Surfer, Ubersuggest, AnswerThePublic, and Google Trends (last 12 months, India).

## Top Insights (Strategic)

### 1. "FREE" is THE winning keyword angle
Google Trends India, last 12 months — top 2 queries:

| Rank | Query | Search Interest | YoY Growth |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | `cheque printing software free` | **100** (peak) | **+70%** |
| 2 | `free cheque printing software` | **99** | **+70%** |

These are also the two **fastest-growing** queries in the entire category. **Cheqify's free positioning is a perfect product–market fit with what users are actively searching for.** This is the #1 keyword pair to win.

### 2. The market leaders are declining — opportunity
Historical incumbents (MoneyFlex, Chrysanth) are losing search interest:

| Competitor | Search Interest | Trend |
|---|---|---|
| `moneyflex` | 29 | **-40%** |
| `moneyflex cheque printing software` | 29 | **-40%** |
| `chrysanth cheque printing software` | 7 | -6% (relative) |
| `chrysanth cheque writer` | 3 | **-30%** |

Both are old desktop-download tools. The market is shifting to web-based / mobile / cloud. Cheqify is positioned for that shift. **High-conversion blog posts: "Cheqify vs MoneyFlex" and "Cheqify vs Chrysanth".**

### 3. Excel is a real fallback users still use
`free cheque printing software in excel` — interest 12, growing **+20%**. Lots of small Indian businesses are still printing cheques from Excel macros. **Blog opportunity: convert them.**

### 4. Long-tail untapped term
`cheque print.in` — interest 5, growing **+60%**. Domain-style search → users are typing a competitor's URL. Worth monitoring; potential branded-defense content.

### 5. Ubersuggest surfaced one big informational query
`cheque number in cheque book` — **2,400 monthly searches**. Pure informational, blog-perfect, near-zero competition for product sites.
