# Product Hunt Launch — Hour-by-Hour Playbook for Cheqify

**Purpose:** Single source of truth for the Product Hunt launch. Walks the 2 weeks before launch + the full launch day + the week after.

**Launch goal:** Top 5 of the day → ~3,000 visits to cheqify.app within 24 hours → ~10 strong backlinks from PH coverage + tech press round-ups.

**Companion files:** `step-4-directory-submissions.md` (cadence + master info), `directory-submission-packets.md` (per-directory ready content).

---

## What Makes a Product Hunt Launch Win

Three things, in order of importance:

1. **Upvotes in the first 4 hours** decide which "Top 5" badge you get (which decides total day traffic).
2. **Quality of the first comment** (yours, posted right after launch) decides how many hunters take you seriously vs. scroll past.
3. **Engagement on every comment** during the day — not just upvotes — feeds the PH algorithm.

Everything below works backward from these three.

---

## Phase 1 — Two Weeks Before Launch (Prep)

### Day -14 to Day -10

**Pick the launch date.** Tuesday or Wednesday in the next 2 weeks. Avoid:
- Mondays (heaviest competition; product backlog cleared)
- Fridays (low engagement)
- US federal holidays (Memorial Day, July 4, Thanksgiving week, Dec 24–Jan 2)
- Indian public holidays where your network is offline (Holi, Diwali week)
- Days when a known YC/AI heavyweight is launching (check `producthunt.com/upcoming` first)

**Create the PH account.** Sign up at producthunt.com with the founder's work email. Verify. Build the founder profile:
- Name, avatar (real photo, not logo)
- One-line bio: "Founder of Cheqify — free cheque printing for Indian SMBs"
- Link to LinkedIn + X
- Upvote 10–20 products you genuinely care about across the next 2 weeks (PH penalises ghost accounts that show up only to launch)

**Build the "Coming Soon" page.** PH lets you create an "upcoming" listing that collects subscribers before launch. Aim for **150+ subscribers** by launch eve.

### Day -10 to Day -3

**Assemble the launch crew.** A core group of 15–25 people who'll upvote + comment within the first 4 hours. Mix of:
- Family members with active PH accounts (not freshly created)
- Existing Cheqify users you've helped personally (warm)
- Founder peers / fellow Indian SaaS founders (reciprocity)
- LinkedIn contacts who'd genuinely care about cheque/fintech tools

**Brief the crew via a private WhatsApp / Telegram group**:
- Launch date and exact time (12:01 AM Pacific = 12:31 PM IST on the launch day)
- A 1-paragraph reminder of what Cheqify does (so their comments are substantive, not "Congrats! 🚀")
- The PH product URL (you'll share it when launch goes live)
- Ask each person to *upvote + leave one specific comment* (not just upvote)

**Critical:** PH detects pump-and-dump upvote clusters. Crew should upvote naturally throughout the first 6 hours, not all in the first 10 minutes.

### Day -3 to Day -1

**Lock in the launch assets:**

- [ ] **Tagline** (max 60 chars): `Free cheque printing software for Indian businesses` *(51/60)*
- [ ] **Description** (max 260 chars): see `directory-submission-packets.md` Section 1
- [ ] **5 screenshots** at 1270×760 px PNG/JPG:
  1. Hero — homepage with main CTA
  2. Cheque printing interface — live MICR validation visible
  3. Bank selection — 300+ layouts grid
  4. Batch printing from Excel — upload + preview
  5. Lifecycle dashboard — issue → cleared timeline
- [ ] **Demo video** — embed `https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DiihuB-hEiY`
- [ ] **First comment** drafted (see template in `directory-submission-packets.md` Section 1, "First comment")
- [ ] **Topics** selected on PH: Finance, Productivity, B2B, SaaS, India

**Schedule the launch on Product Hunt.** PH lets you "submit and schedule" the post for a specific day. Submit at least 24 hours before launch to give Maker Studio reviewers time to approve. Choose **12:01 AM Pacific** (12:31 PM IST) — this is when the leaderboard resets.

**Pre-write social posts** for the launch hour (don't write them mid-launch):
- LinkedIn: 3–4 sentence post with PH URL, asking for support
- X (Twitter): one tweet + one quote-tweet of the PH post
- WhatsApp status / story: PH screenshot + URL
- Founder peer DMs: 5–10 personal messages to top supporters

## Phase 2 — Launch Day (Hour by Hour)

All times in IST (India Standard Time) since the founder is in Surat. PH operates on PT.

### 12:30 PM IST — Launch goes live (PH 12:01 AM PT)

**Minute 0:** PH listing flips public. *You* are the first upvote — vote on your own product (PH allows and expects this).

**Minute 1–5:** Post your **first comment** (the founder intro from the packets file). This sets the conversational tone for the whole day.

**Minute 5–15:**
- Share the PH URL in the launch crew's WhatsApp/Telegram group with a single sentence: "We're live! Please upvote + leave any short comment."
- Post on LinkedIn
- Post on X
- DM your top 5 supporters individually

**Minute 15–60:** Reply to every comment that comes in. Don't just thank — engage with their specific point. PH's algorithm reads response depth.

### 1:30 PM IST (1 hour post-launch)

**Target:** ~30 upvotes, 5–10 comments. If you're at this, you're on track for Top 10.

If you're below this, send a softer follow-up to the crew: "Going strong — anyone who hasn't dropped by yet, this is the time."

### 3:00 PM IST (2.5 hours post-launch)

**Target:** ~80 upvotes. Top 5 cutoff usually settles between 200–400 upvotes by end of day depending on the day's competition.

**Engagement tactic:** Reply to every comment with a question back. Forces re-engagement and tells the algorithm the discussion is alive.

### 5:00 PM IST (4.5 hours post-launch)

**Target:** ~150 upvotes. By this point your leaderboard position for the day is mostly locked in (algorithm-wise — final tally is at end of day Pacific).

**Strategic move:** Reach out to 2–3 newsletter writers / startup curators who cover Indian SaaS. A live PH listing gives them a hook. Examples:
- **The Ken's Pulse Indian SaaS column** — submit via their tip form
- **Indiehackers post** — write a "We launched on PH today" short post linking to your PH listing
- Indian startup subreddits (r/IndianStartups, r/IndianStreetBets won't fit) — share the PH link with context

### 9:00 PM IST (8.5 hours post-launch)

**Target:** ~250 upvotes. Top 5 is in reach. Activity often slows mid-day Pacific (mid-morning US) — your Indian timezone advantage is the 5–9 PM IST window which overlaps with PM-launch Pacific viewers waking up.

**Send a second round to crew members who haven't upvoted yet.** Specifically ask them to leave a comment — even a one-liner — not just an upvote.

### 11:30 PM IST (11 hours post-launch)

**Target:** ~350+ upvotes for solid Top 5 placement.

By now: thank every commenter (PH community values founders who reply to every single comment). If you've replied to ALL comments and the day is winding down, you're in good shape.

### 11:59 AM IST next day (24 hours, PH day ends)

PH's "Product of the Day" is decided at 12:00 AM PT (the moment the next day starts). Final placement is locked.

**If Top 5:** screenshot the badge, post on LinkedIn + X with thank-you to the community, archive the PH listing URL for "as featured on" social proof.

**If not Top 5:** still archive the badge. Even a #6–10 finish drives meaningful traffic and credible "Launched on Product Hunt" social proof.

## Phase 3 — The Week After Launch

### Day +1

- Reply to any late comments. PH listings keep getting views for weeks.
- Share post-launch numbers on LinkedIn: "Yesterday on PH we got X upvotes, Y signups, Z conversations — thank you 🙏"
- Email the launch crew with a short thank-you note. Mention if anyone wants beta access to upcoming features.

### Day +3

- Email any tech press / newsletter contacts who picked up the launch (or didn't but might still cover it). Subject line: "We hit #N on Product Hunt — would you cover Cheqify?"
- Reach out to writers at **Inc42**, **YourStory**, **The Ken**, **The Hindu BusinessLine** with a soft pitch.

### Day +7

- Post a "1 week post-launch" recap on LinkedIn / Medium. Real numbers, what worked, what didn't, what's next.
- Submit to the next batch of directories from `step-4-directory-submissions.md` cadence (AlternativeTo, SaaSHub, Capterra).
- Update `directory-submission-packets.md` Submission Tracking Sheet with the PH listing URL.

### Day +30

- Revisit GSC: did the PH backlink + the resulting media mentions move any search rankings?
- Check PostHog for the conversion funnel of users who came via `?utm_source=producthunt`.
- If a follow-up product update is ready, you can do a **"Cheqify 2.0 — back on PH"** launch in 6+ months (PH allows re-launches at 6-month intervals if you have a major update).

## What Could Go Wrong (And How to Recover)

**Site goes down from PH traffic:** Netlify handles the static export well, but blog dynamic routes via Sanity may hit the Sanity rate limit. Pre-launch, warm the cache for the homepage and feature page. Have a static fallback ready.

**Negative comment thread:** Reply factually, never defensively. If someone says "this is just MoneyFlex with a free tier" — agree it's similar in surface, then explain the printable-cheque + lifecycle + free-forever positioning. Never delete comments.

**A bigger product launches the same day:** Won't kill you if your crew shows up. The Top 5 typically has space for product-and-product-and-product mixes. If a YC AI launch is dominating, just aim for Top 10 — still drives real traffic.

**You don't make Top 5:** That's fine. Even Top 20 of the day drives ~500–1500 visits and a PH backlink. Save the launch URL, screenshot the position, treat it as social proof going forward.

## Estimated Outcome (Realistic, Not Aspirational)

For a free SaaS in a niche category (cheque printing) with a focused India audience:

| Outcome | Likelihood | What it means |
|---|---|---|
| Top 5 of the day | 30–40% | Best case — 3000+ visits, 30+ signups, strong PH badge |
| Top 10 of the day | 50–60% | Solid — 1500+ visits, 15+ signups, badge still meaningful |
| Top 20 of the day | 80%+ | Floor — 500+ visits, useful PH backlink, archived for "as featured on" |
| Below Top 20 | 15–20% | Crew didn't show; consider re-launch in 6+ months with a major update |

The base case (Top 10) is enough to justify the prep work. Anything better is a bonus.

## Last Note

This is a launch, not a campaign. The PH traffic spike resolves in ~48 hours. The lasting value is:

1. The backlink from producthunt.com (high domain authority)
2. The "Featured on Product Hunt" social proof (use on website, in emails, in pitches)
3. The 1–2 newsletter / blog pickups that come from a strong launch
4. The 5–10 early adopters who become real users

The 1500 visits on launch day are nice. The 5 long-term users you keep are the actual return.
