## 4A. Global SaaS Directories (Highest Priority — Do First)

### 1. Product Hunt — `https://www.producthunt.com/`
- **Why:** Massive traffic spike on launch day; SEO backlink from PH; tech-press picks up trending posts.
- **Step 1:** Sign up at `producthunt.com` with your work email. Verify the account.
- **Step 2:** Click your avatar → "Submit a product" → fill in:
  - Name, tagline, description (use values above)
  - Topics: pick **Finance**, **Productivity**, **SaaS**, **B2B**
  - Upload 4–5 screenshots (homepage hero, features, comparison, pricing) — 1270×760 px
  - Add the demo YouTube link
  - Choose a launch date (pick a Tuesday or Wednesday in the next 2 weeks — best engagement days)
- **Strategy:** Upvote your own product first thing 12:01 AM PST on launch day. Share to your LinkedIn, X, WhatsApp groups. Aim for 50+ upvotes in first 4 hours to crack the daily leaderboard.
- **Status:** [ ] Pending

### 2. AlternativeTo — `https://alternativeto.net/`
- **Why:** Ranks for "[competitor] alternative" Google searches. Free SEO win.
- **Step 1:** Sign up.
- **Step 2:** Search "ChequePot" → if it exists, click "Add an alternative" → submit Cheqify. If ChequePot doesn't exist, first submit ChequePot (with link to their site), then add Cheqify as an alternative.
- **Step 3:** Also submit as alternative to: **MoneyFlex**, **Chrysanth Cheque Writer**, **Versacheck**.
- **Status:** [ ] Pending

## Best Practices (Read Before Submitting)

1. **Use the same product name everywhere:** Cheqify (not Cheqify.app, not "Cheqify - Cheque Printing"). Brand consistency = SEO entity authority.

2. **Use the same logo and screenshots everywhere.** Visual consistency reinforces brand recognition.

3. **Do NOT mass-submit on the same day.** Spread 3–4 per week. Google considers rapid backlink growth a spam signal.

4. **Check existing listings first.** Some directories may already have Cheqify if anyone (you, an early user, a competitor) submitted it. Search before duplicating.

5. **Track every submission URL** in this checklist. You'll need them later for:
   - Disavow checks (if any spam directory backfires)
   - Pitch leverage ("featured on G2, Capterra, ProductHunt")
   - Analytics referrals (UTM tags optional)

6. **Add `?utm_source=producthunt&utm_medium=listing` etc.** to your website URL in each listing — gives you traffic-source visibility in PostHog.

7. **Reviews matter.** After listing on G2 / Capterra / GetApp, email your top 5–10 happy users and ask them to leave a review. Without reviews, listings rank near the bottom of category pages.
