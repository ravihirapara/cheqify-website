# Story 6.3: Blog Seed Content

Status: done

## Story

As a visitor,
I want useful blog content available at launch,
so that I can discover Cheqify through SEO search results.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. **Given** the blog is live, **When** a visitor browses, **Then** at least 3 seed articles are available in all 3 languages
2. Article 1: "How to Print a Cheque at Home"
3. Article 2: "Cheque Bounce: Reasons and Solutions"
4. Article 3: "Cheqify vs Manual Cheque Writing"
5. **Given** each article, **When** viewed, **Then** proper frontmatter exists (title, description, date, author, slug, lang, tags, image)

## Dev Notes

- Content from product brief — these are the 3 defined seed articles
- Each article in all 3 languages at content/blog/{en,hi,gu}/{slug}.md
- Target SEO keywords: "cheque printing", "cheque bounce", "how to print cheque"
- Author: Cheqify Team
