Cheqify is a free cheque printing and lifecycle management platform built specifically for Indian small and medium businesses. It eliminates the most common causes of bounced cheques — handwriting errors, wrong MICR positioning, and incorrect amount-in-words formatting — by handling everything automatically.

Why businesses choose Cheqify:

• 300+ Indian bank formats pre-loaded — SBI, HDFC, ICICI, Axis, PNB, Kotak, Yes Bank, IDFC First, Federal Bank, and hundreds more. No need to manually configure cheque layouts; just select your bank.

• MICR validation — the encoded numbers at the bottom of every cheque must be positioned with sub-millimeter accuracy. Cheqify gets this right every time.

• Batch printing — load 50 cheque leaves, upload your payee list (CSV or Excel), and print them all in one go. Saves hours every payroll cycle.

• Lifecycle tracking — know instantly which cheques have been deposited, cleared, returned, or are still outstanding. Get auto-reminders for post-dated cheques. Maintain a complete audit trail.

• Owner dashboard — get a bird's-eye view across all your projects, branches, or business units. Monitor team activity, outstanding payments, and cash flow in one place.

Use cases:
- Vendor and supplier payments
- Salary disbursement (especially for SMBs not yet on bank transfers)
- Rent, EMI, and recurring post-dated cheques
- Statutory payments (advance tax, GST, PF challans)
- Trust and society fund disbursements

Cheqify is built and maintained by CodeTailor Software, a Surat-based product company. Already trusted by 2,000+ businesses who have printed over 50,000 cheques.

## Tips before submitting

1. **Don't submit all in one day** — Google sees a sudden burst of new backlinks as suspicious. Do 2–3 per week over 2 months.
2. **Product Hunt launch**: pick a Tuesday or Wednesday, post at 12:01 AM PST. Have 5–10 friends ready to upvote in first hour.
3. **Use the same product name, tagline, and primary screenshot** across all directories — consistency helps brand recall.
4. **G2 / Capterra / GetApp**: ask 5–10 happy customers to leave reviews after you list. Reviews matter more than the listing itself.
5. **Get the dofollow status**: a "dofollow" backlink passes SEO authority; "nofollow" doesn't. Most SaaS directories are dofollow; most government/news sites are nofollow.
